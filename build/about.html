Hi! I am Mathis Randl, a PhD student in the SaCS lab at EPFL. I do research at the intersection of databases and machine learning, with a focus on improving the scalability and efficiency of large-scale inference.

These days, I'm (for the most part) working on software techniques for approximate vector search. Systems based on exotic hardware for vector retrieval are also in the works.

This website is meant to store my CV/resume, as well as archive papers, blog posts and announcements related to my professional works. Feel free to explore around!
